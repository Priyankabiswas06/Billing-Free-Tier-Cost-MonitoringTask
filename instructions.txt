Free Tier Alerts - Quick Steps (Console)
1. Sign in to the AWS Console as the account root user or an IAM user with billing access.
2. Go to 'Billing' from the AWS Management Console (top-right menu under your account).
3. In the left navigation, choose 'Free Tier'.
4. Click 'Enable' or 'Set up Free Tier alerts' and provide an email to receive notifications.
5. Save and confirm any subscription email you receive (if applicable).
